SHORT BIO

Since September 2019, I am a Ph.D. student at IRIF in Paris supervised by Benedikt Bollig and Arnaud Sangnier. I defended my Ph.D. on the 14th December 2023. Before that, I studied computer science at ENS Cachan (now ENS Paris-Saclay). More in the past, I studied pure mathematics at Orsay University (now Paris-Saclay University).
